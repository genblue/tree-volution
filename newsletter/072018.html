TREE-volution Newsletter

Dear friend,

We hope you are enjoying the blossoming of Springtime. Many moons have passed since our last newsletter, and there is much to share.

With your support, over the last year Worldview International Foundation (WIF) has planted over 1 million new mangroves in Thor Heyerdahl Climate Park. These mangroves will sequester 1 million tonnes of CO2 over the next twenty years, and produce community and biodiversity co-benefits 1000 times the initial investment.

In the coming months, we are planting 2 million more mangrove trees, continuing the world's first mangrove drone project, and welcoming a new generation of mangrove protectors.

We thank you for being part of the TREE-volution community and helping to build a global movement to protect and restore critically endangered mangroves.

Stay tuned for mangroovy updates.

May the forest be with you,

Arne Fjortoft
Secretary General
Worldview International Foundation

Planting 2 Million Mangrove Trees in 2018

Pictured above are 600,000 new climate protectors in our nursery ready for action. They are reinforced by 1.4 million propagules for planting 2 million mangrove trees in the next few months. This will add great value to our livelihood program by reducing poverty and in mitigating CO2. Welcome to a new season of hope.  

Continuing The First Mangrove Drone Project

Comprehensive drone planting for mangrove restoration successfully completed recently by WIF in Myanmar. This pioneering project has created keen interest among the international development community, including the new Executive Director of UN Environment, Erik Solheim, the chief Minister of Yangon, and prominent TREE corporate partners Generation Blue and Starboard.
