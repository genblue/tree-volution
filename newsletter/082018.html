WIF Just Got Verified

WIF is the first mangrove project to be registered with Verified Carbon Standard in Myanmar. This is another milestone in our common effort to address climate change with facts and international recognition. RINA, a leading company in climate verification projects, has just concluded its audit. Trading will start in August for all the CO2 emitted from Thor Heyerdahl Climate Park. Communities will greatly benefit from the proceeds. This project is more than just tree planting. It creates economic security and provides a better life for local communities too.
